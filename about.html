Our Story

Be You Art is a kid-made art company and website. We are here to make art and share it with the world.

Be You Art started with an axolotl drawing, and then I wanted to draw more animals I love. I made more animal art, and then realized I could sell it and donate part of the money to help endangered animals.

Next, I asked my older brother to make me a website so we could sell it together. Now, you can purchase customizable art for birthdays and holidays or just because you love animals too! And that’s our story.
